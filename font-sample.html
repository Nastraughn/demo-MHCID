Once upon a time, before MHCID C4...

There was a young man studying economics and psychology at an Ivy League institution, because he thought it would help him make money to pay off his student loans.

"Huzzah!" he cried when he accepted his first job offer in the lovely San Francisco Bay Area.

But allas, he quickly realized that 'twas not the field for him.

So, he went back to his roots. He began drawing and sketching product concepts again. Like he used to do before being jaded by classmates seeking high-paying jobs on Wall Street. And before he knew it, he was accepted and enrolled in the MHCID program at UC Irvine!
